Bienvenido a JavaScriptSpanish


Explore Spanish translation and grammar through a sampling of the developer's favorite Spanish-language poems, whether or not this is something out of your high school nightmares.


Poems of this collection are pulled from resources and sites such as la Universidad de Chile and the Poetry Foundation. Translations are a combination of these same resources and the developer's own interpretations.
